Leitermann middleware for article distribution

For our client, I was involved in implementing various marketplace extensions, including Ebay, Amazon, Kaufland, Aimondo and generic CSV exports. We created a middleware that is connected to our own software product Online Media Net and retrieves articles from there, enriches them with additional information or formats them, depending on the configuration, and then routes them out.

In the process, I also made several other backend optimizations.
