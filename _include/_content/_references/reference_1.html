Ninja Elefant

Ninja Elefant is the first game in 3D based on the Unity-Engine, which was developed during the GameDevWeek in Wintersemester 2016/2017. In this stealth-game you are controlling an Ninja-Elefant through a museum and must avoid guardians an traps.
