I already implemented the boid behaviour in the Unity-Engine and contributed it to a University-Game-Project as a port to the Unreal-Engine. And used it for a game mechanic where the player can release fireflies from the lantern and control them, this allows a more realistic behaviour of the swarm that is following and they also doge obstacles. Their behaviour can also be modified dependent of other logic.
