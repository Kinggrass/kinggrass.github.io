Pneumatic Man is a classic platformer game developed during the GameDevWeek at Hochschule Trier. The goal in each level is to rescueue your mates and reach the end.

Under following link you will find the official wiki entry, there you can download the game or source-code. https://fsi.hochschule-trier.de/dev/null/Pneumatic_Man
